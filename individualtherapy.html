Individual Therapy

Concerns I Frequently Support

  • Depression, anxiety, and stress
  • Relationship patterns and emotional wellbeing
  • Self-esteem, confidence, and body image concerns
  • Trauma, grief, and major life transitions
  • OCD, phobias, and panic-related difficulties
  • Anger regulation and behavioural patterns
  • ADHD and attention-related challenges
  • Eating-related concerns and unhealthy coping patterns
  • Career dissatisfaction, purpose, and identity concerns
  • Loneliness, isolation, and reconnecting with oneself

Additional Areas of Clinical Focus

  • Performance-related concerns including performance anxiety
  • Cultural and identity-related stressors
  • Specific fears such as flying or medical anxiety
  • Behavioural patterns affecting daily functioning
  • Concerns linked to chronic stress or emotional overwhelm
  • Adjustment concerns after major life changes

How I Work

Each session is 50 minutes, offered in-person or online — a structured, supportive, and grounded therapeutic space centred entirely around you.

Therapeutic Techniques

  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)
  • Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Schema Therapy
  • EMDR
  • Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)
  • Exposure & Response Prevention (ERP)
  • Interpersonal & Social Rhythm Therapy
  • Compassion-Focused Therapy (CFT)
  • Mindfulness-Based Interventions
  • Motivational Enhancement Therapy (MET)

Most people come in for a specific reason — but stay because something shifts. When you're ready, I’m here to support you through that journey.

BENEFITS

What You Can Expect

Therapy with me is calm, collaborative, and paced with intention. Whether we meet in-person or online, you can expect a space where your thoughts are taken seriously, your feelings are met with respect, and your experiences are never rushed or minimised. My role is to listen closely, offer clarity when things feel tangled, and bring in evidence-based approaches in a way that feels clear and practical.
