About Us

About Aravinda

We all carry silent battles. Some are loud and visible, but most live quietly within us in our anxieties, our relationships, or in the quiet moments when we feel unlike ourselves. Therapy is where the unspoken can find expression, and I am here to help you pause, reflect, and move through these moments with clarity. I am Aravinda Nandakumar, a Counselling and Family Therapist trained in advanced psychotherapies. I work with individuals, couples, and families who are facing challenges such as anxiety, grief, trauma, and relational conflict.

Professional Background

I hold an M.Sc. in Counselling and Family Therapy (First Class with Distinction, IGNOU), and have continued to deepen my expertise through advanced certifications from institutions such as NIMHANS, the American Psychological Association (APA), and the University of Pennsylvania. My expertise spans Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, mindfulness, relationship and pre-marital counselling, substance abuse work, abnormal psychology, positive psychology, and trauma recovery. I continually review new research and emerging therapeutic developments, integrating effective practices so the support you receive is nuanced and clinically refined. My clinical experience includes my time at Spandana Rehabilitation Centre, where I conducted psychological assessments and supported patients navigating depression, substance dependence, and complex family dynamics.
